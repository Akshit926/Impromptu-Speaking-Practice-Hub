Word of the Day

Perspicacious

adjective /ˌpər-spə-ˈkā-shəs/

Definition

Having a ready insight into and understanding of things.

Synonyms

shrewd, perceptive, astute

Example

"It offers quite a few facts to the perspicacious reporter."
